Brief History Of Nigeria

Visited by Europeans in the 15th century, it became a centre for the trade in enslaved people. The area came under British control in 1861 and was made a British colony in 1914. Nigeria gained independence in 1960 and became a republic in 1963. In the late 17th to early 18th centuries, Oyo was at its zenith and expanded its influence from western Nigeria to present-day Togo. The Benin Empire had sovereignty over the region between the 15th and 19th centuries.
